## 🐾 About Me

Heya, I’m **n3kuri**.

I’m a **Roblox developer** and **game tester** who enjoys creating clean, useful, and easy-to-use systems. I like working on Roblox assets, scripts, tools, UI improvements, and gameplay features that make games feel smoother and more polished.

I also enjoy testing games, finding bugs, improving player experience, and learning new ways to make my projects better. Outside of development, I love anime and usually bring a bit of catboy energy into my style.
